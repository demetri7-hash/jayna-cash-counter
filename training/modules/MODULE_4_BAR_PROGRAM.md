# UNIT 4.1: SPIRITS 101 ‚Äî FOUNDATIONS

**Duration:** 3-4 hours | **Trainer:** Kayla

## The Six Base Spirits

### VODKA
- **Source:** Grains or potatoes
- **Profile:** Clean, neutral, smooth
- **Uses:** Martinis, Moscow Mules, Kahvetini
- **Our bottle:** Hanson of Sonoma Organic

### GIN
- **Source:** Neutral spirit + botanicals (juniper)
- **Profile:** Herbal, piney, citrus
- **Uses:** G&T, Martini, Negroni

### RUM
- **Source:** Sugarcane or molasses
- **Profile:** Sweet, caramel, tropical
- **Types:** White, Gold, Dark, Spiced

### TEQUILA
- **Source:** Blue agave (Mexico)
- **Profile:** Earthy, vegetal, peppery
- **Types:** Blanco, Reposado, A√±ejo
- **Our bottle:** Lalo (used in multiple cocktails)

### WHISKEY
- **Source:** Fermented grain, aged in barrels
- **Profile:** Vanilla, caramel, spice, smoke
- **Types:** Bourbon, Rye, Scotch, Irish
- **Our bottle:** Elijah Craig Bourbon

### BRANDY
- **Source:** Distilled wine/fruit
- **Profile:** Rich, fruity, warming

## Greek/Mediterranean Spirits

### RAKI (Turkish)
- Anise-flavored, turns cloudy with water
- Infused with dill for Yamas!

### OUZO (Greek)
- Greek anise spirit
- Served as welcome/celebration shot

### MASTIHA / KLEOS (Greek)
- Made from mastic resin (Chios island)
- Pine, cedar, herbal‚Äîutterly unique
- Star of Myth + Aloe

# UNIT 4.3: COCKTAIL THEORY & TECHNIQUE

**Duration:** 3-4 hours | **Trainer:** Kayla

## Cocktail Families

**SOURS** (Spirit + Citrus + Sweet)
- Formula: 2 oz spirit + 1 oz citrus + ¬æ oz sweet
- Technique: SHAKE (citrus = shake)

**SPIRIT-FORWARD** (Spirit + Modifier)
- Formula: 2 oz spirit + ¬Ω-1 oz modifier
- Technique: STIR (no citrus = stir)

**HIGHBALLS** (Spirit + Mixer)
- Formula: 1.5-2 oz spirit + 4-6 oz mixer
- Technique: BUILD in glass

## Technique Fundamentals

### SHAKING
- When: Citrus, dairy, eggs
- How: Ice-filled tin, shake hard 10-15 seconds
- Goal: Chill, dilute, integrate

### STIRRING
- When: Spirit-forward, no citrus
- How: Mixing glass, bar spoon, 30-45 seconds
- Goal: Chill, dilute, keep silky

### MUDDLING
- Herbs: Gentle press‚Äîrelease oils, don't shred
- Fruit: Firmer‚Äîextract juice

### DRY SHAKE
- When: Egg white or foamer
- How: Shake WITHOUT ice first (emulsify), then add ice

### STRAINING
- Hawthorne: Spring-loaded, fits shaker
- Fine mesh: Double-strain for no ice chips

## Glassware
- Coupe: Shaken cocktails, up drinks
- Rocks: Spirit-forward on ice
- Highball: Tall drinks with mixer
- Clay pot: Yamas! (our signature)

## Mise en Place
Before service: Ice full, juices checked, batch cocktails ready, garnishes prepped, tools positioned

# UNIT 4.6: RESPONSIBLE SERVICE & RECOMMENDATIONS

**Duration:** 2-3 hours | **Trainer:** Kayla + Demetri

## California RBS Requirements
- Must be 21+ to drink
- Servers can be personally liable
- RBS certification required within 60 days

## Signs of Intoxication
- Slurred speech
- Loss of coordination
- Glassy eyes
- Overly loud/emotional

## Slowing Service
- "Let me get you some water"
- "Can I get you some food?"
- Take longer between drinks

## Cutting Off
Firm, kind, clear:
- "I'm closing your tab‚Äîwant to make sure everyone gets home safe"
- Never say "drunk" or "too much"
- Get manager if pushback

## Drink Recommendations
