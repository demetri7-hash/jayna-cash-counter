üìä How It Works (Simple Version)

The system watches three things:

1. What You Have
Current stock in your kitchen (you count it daily)
2. What You Use
How fast items run out between counts
3. What You Need
Par levels (minimum amounts to keep)
4. Patterns
Busy days, slow days, seasonal changes

Result: Smart recommendations that get better over time as the system learns your unique patterns.

üîÑ Daily Counting Workflow

STEP 1: Start a new count session (give it a name like "Monday Morning" or "Weekend Prep")
STEP 2: Walk through your kitchen/storage and count each item
STEP 3: Enter the numbers (system saves automatically)
STEP 4: Review the Prep Sheet - shows what's running low
STEP 5: Use recommendations to create vendor orders

üß† How the System Learns (Smart Features)

1. Consumption Tracking

Every time you count, the system calculates how much was used since the last count. Example:

Monday: 10 CAMBRO of salad mix
Wednesday: 4 CAMBRO of salad mix
‚Üí System learns: You use ~3 CAMBRO per day

2. Pattern Recognition

After 2-3 weeks, the system notices patterns:

  • Weekends use 2x more chicken than weekdays
  • Arugula runs out faster on Friday/Saturday
  • Mondays are slower ‚Üí less consumption

3. Smart Recommendations

Based on patterns, it suggests optimal order amounts:

Basic calculation: Par level - Current stock = Order amount
Smart calculation: Considers consumption rate, days until next order, busy periods, historical patterns ‚Üí Better recommendation

‚ùì Common Questions

Q: Why is the recommendation different from (Par - Current)?

A: The system considers how fast you're using the item. If you use 5 CAMBRO per day and won't order again for 3 days, it suggests more than just reaching par level.

Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?

A: You can always adjust quantities manually. Over time, the system will learn from your adjustments.

Q: How long until the AI is accurate?

A: Basic accuracy: 3-5 days. High accuracy: 2-3 weeks. Full pattern recognition: 4-6 weeks.

Q: What happens if I skip a day of counting?

A: The system adjusts automatically, but consistency helps it learn faster and make better predictions.

Q: Can I use this on my phone?

A: Yes! The system works on any device with a web browser. Perfect for counting with a tablet while walking through the kitchen.
