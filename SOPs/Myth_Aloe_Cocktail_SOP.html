KLEOS Mastiha Spirit (1.5 oz)

What It Is:

  • Greek mastiha liqueur made from the resin of the mastiha tree (mastic tree)
  • Grown exclusively on the Greek island of Chios - nowhere else in the world
  • Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) product
  • The name "KLEOS" means "glory" or "renown" in ancient Greek

The Mastiha Story:

  • Mastiha resin has been harvested on Chios for over 2,500 years
  • Ancient Greeks chewed it as the world's first chewing gum
  • Used in traditional medicine, cooking, and religious ceremonies
  • The trees "cry" tears of resin that are hand-harvested

Taste Profile:

  • Herbal and pine-like with subtle sweetness
  • Notes of: cedar, mint, eucalyptus, and anise
  • Slightly earthy, resinous finish
  • Clean, crisp alcohol base (30% ABV)
Staff Talking Points:
"KLEOS is a Greek spirit made from mastiha‚Äîa rare tree resin that only grows on one Greek island, Chios. It's been harvested there for over 2,500 years! The name means 'glory' in ancient Greek, which is exactly what this drink delivers. It has this amazing herbal, pine-like flavor that's both refreshing and mysterious."

Chareau Aloe Liqueur (0.75 oz)

What It Is:

  • Handcrafted with fresh aloe vera, cucumber, lemon peel, muskmelon, and spearmint

üìã THE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz KLEOS Mastiha Spirit
  • 0.5 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 oz Simple syrup
  • 0.75 oz Chareau Aloe Liqueur
  • 1 lemon rind (peeled top to bottom with peeler)
  • Sugar (for rim)
  • Lemon twist (for garnish)

Equipment Needed:

  • Muddler
  • Coupe glass
  • Cocktail shaker
  • Hawthorne strainer
  • Small plate (for sugar rim)
  • Vegetable peeler or channel knife
  • Jigger

üë®‚Äçüç≥ PREPARATION STEPS

STEP 1: Prepare the Garnished Glass

  1. Sugar rim: Pour sugar onto a small plate
  2. Wet the rim of a coupe glass with lemon juice or water (only half the rim)
  3. Dip the wet portion into sugar to create "a kiss of sugar" - not the full rim
  4. Twist lemon peel over the glass to spray citrus oils onto the inside
  5. Drape the lemon twist on the rim as garnish
  6. Set aside while you build the drink

STEP 2: Build the Cocktail

  1. Muddle: Take 1 lemon rind (peeled top to bottom) and place in shaker
  2. Add 1 oz simple syrup to the shaker
  3. Muddle firmly to extract citrus oils from the rind (10-15 seconds)
  4. Add the remaining ingredients:
    • 1.5 oz KLEOS
    • 0.5 oz fresh lemon juice
    • 0.75 oz Chareau
  5. Fill shaker with ice

STEP 3: Shake & Serve

  1. Shake vigorously until "bruised" (well-chilled, about 15-20 seconds)
  2. You should see frost on the outside of the shaker
  3. Strain into the prepared coupe glass
  4. Serve immediately

üéØ PRESENTATION & SERVICE

The Perfect Serve:

  • Glass: Chilled coupe
  • Garnish: Lemon twist draped elegantly on rim
  • Rim: Half-sugared rim ("a kiss of sugar" - not the entire rim)
  • Color: Pale cloudy citrus-yellow
  • Temperature: Ice cold
