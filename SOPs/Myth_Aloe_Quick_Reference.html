MYTH + ALOE

Jayna Gyro Signature Cocktail

üìã THE RECIPE

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz KLEOS Mastiha Spirit
  • 0.5 oz Fresh lemon juice
  • 1 oz Simple syrup
  • 0.75 oz Chareau Aloe Liqueur
  • 1 lemon rind (peeler, top to bottom)
  • Sugar for rim, lemon twist garnish

üë®‚Äçüç≥ PREPARATION

STEP 1: Prepare Glass

  1. Half-rim coupe glass with sugar ("kiss of sugar")
  2. Twist lemon peel over glass, spray oils
  3. Drape twist on rim, set aside

STEP 2: Build Cocktail

  1. Muddle lemon rind + 1 oz simple (10-15 sec)
  2. Add KLEOS, lemon juice, Chareau
  3. Fill with ice

STEP 3: Shake & Serve

  1. Shake vigorously until "bruised" (15-20 sec)
  2. Strain into prepared coupe
  3. Serve immediately

üçã KEY INGREDIENTS

KLEOS Mastiha (1.5 oz)

Greek spirit from mastiha tree resin grown exclusively on Chios island for 2,500+ years. "KLEOS" = glory in ancient Greek. Taste: herbal, pine-like, cedar, mint, eucalyptus, anise. 30% ABV.

Chareau Aloe Liqueur (0.75 oz)

California aloe vera liqueur with fresh aloe, cucumber, lemon, melon, spearmint. Handcrafted in LA. Taste: light, vegetal, refreshing, silky texture. 20% ABV.
